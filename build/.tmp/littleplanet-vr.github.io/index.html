What is LittleVRPlanet?

"LittleVRPlanet wants to offer the VR glass in low cost along with a wide platform full of educational and entertainment content for kids."

How are we going to get?

We want to make it cheaper developing a mobile replacement device. Nowadays, smartphones with virtual reality support are expensive that’s why enjoying virtual reality is not allowed to everybody.
